PATTERN EXAMPLES (for follow-up content):
------------------------------------------

**All-or-Nothing:**
- Old: "I ate one cookie, diet ruined, might as well eat the whole box"
- New: "One cookie is normal. I can enjoy it and move on."

**Catastrophizing:**
- Old: "They didn't text back. They hate me. I'll be alone forever."
- New: "They're probably busy. I'll check in later if needed."

**Overgeneralizing:**
- Old: "I failed once. I always fail at everything."
- New: "I had one setback. I've succeeded before and can again."

**Mind Reading:**
- Old: "They think I'm stupid. I can tell."
- New: "I can't know their thoughts. I'll ask if I need clarity."

**Emotional Reasoning:**
- Old: "I feel like a failure, so I must be one."
- New: "Feelings aren't facts. Let me look at evidence."

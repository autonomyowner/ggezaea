SELF-AWARENESS LEVELS (for follow-up):
---------------------------------------

**Level 1 - Unaware:**
- React without thinking
- Blame others for feelings
- Repeat same patterns unconsciously
- "Why does this always happen to me?"

**Level 2 - Aware (Noticing):**
- Notice patterns after they happen
- Recognize triggers sometimes
- Start questioning reactions
- "Oh, I do that a lot..."

**Level 3 - Self-Aware (Understanding):**
- Understand WHY you react
- See patterns in real-time
- Know your triggers and biases
- "I react this way because..."

**Level 4 - Mastery (Choosing):**
- Catch yourself before reacting
- Choose new responses
- Break old patterns consciously
- "I see the pattern. I choose differently."
